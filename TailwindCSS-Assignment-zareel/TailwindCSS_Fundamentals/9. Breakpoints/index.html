Himeji Castle Cherry Blossoms

The picture-perfect way to see Himeji Castle


In late-March to mid-April every year, the cherry trees that surround Himeji Castle bloom spectacularly, transforming the famous castle into one of Japan's premier cherry blossom viewing spots. How to Get There


Himeji Castle is a 20-minute walk along Otemaedori Street or a five-minute bus ride from Himeji Station. Himeji Station is connected to Tokyo , Osaka , Hiroshima and other major cities via the Tokaido-Sanyo Shinkansen.
